Top 10 Worst Passwords in Circulation

This chart visualizes the absolute worst passwords globally based on billions of public data breach records. Ensure you never use any of these.

Why is "123456" so common?

It's primarily due to human psychology leaning towards convenience over security, combined with systems that lack minimum complexity enforcement. Botnets can crack these in literally 0.00 seconds.

The Ripple Effect

When users reuse one of these top strings on multiple sites, a single database breach compromises their identity everywhere instantly. This is known as Credential Stuffing.
